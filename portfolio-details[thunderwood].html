GOAL: Design a quiz-taking flow for a new university

Thunderwood University (located in Easley, SC) has asked us to create a webpage for their website that allows computer science students to study specific materials. Many students are studying Web Development, which includes mastering the three fundamental languages: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The webpage should consist of links to several practice quiz questions that students can easily access. These quizzes should offer questions that help students better study the programming languages offered at the school. After completing any of the following quizzes, students should be able to view quiz results (such as number of questions missed, summary of results, etc.). The university would also like the webpage to be designed for mobile devices as well.
