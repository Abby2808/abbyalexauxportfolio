  I was born, raised, and currently live in Easley, South Carolina. I graduated from Powdersville High School in May 2018. I went to college not knowing what I wanted to do as a career. However, after completing 2 years at Greenville Technical College as a transfer student, I decided to pursue a career as a Front-End Web Developer. I became very involved in coding and loved completing web projects for school. I completed my education at Tricounty Technical College and in August 2022, I earned my A.S. in Computer Science with a concentration in Software Development.

From my internship span from September to December, I got the opportunity to work alongside Front-End Developers and UX Designers. Since then, I've been applying to numerous jobs. Although I haven't been able to get a real interview, I have been building both my resume and skill set with various certifications and online classes. I've recently completed the Google UX Design Professional Certification course and it has truly opened my mind to UX Designing.
