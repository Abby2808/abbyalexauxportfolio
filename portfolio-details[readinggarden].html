GOAL: Design a cross-platform service to help kids and teens find books that they would enjoy reading

Reading Garden is a new website and mobile app inspired by the ideas of school teachers to help children and teenagers who dislike reading. This app is designed to help kids of all ages explore different genres to find the perfect books that would grab they're interest and begin to grow their imaginations. The book recommendations are sorted by grade levels (from kindergarten to high school) and can be easily navigated either alone or with the help of a parent/teacher. The app is not only for children, but also includes blogs for parents, school portals for teachers, and a contact page to ask any questions regarding the site's information.
